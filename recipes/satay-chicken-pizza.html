Satay Chicken Pizza

This recipe is the result of a deep hunger and a shallow pantry. But with a little creativity this great dish was born.

Ingredients

  • 2 skinless, boneless chicken breast halves, chopped
  • 1 cup Satay peanut sauce
  • 1 bunch green onions chopped
  • 4 small pita breads
  • slices of provolone cheese
  • 1 tablespoon vegetable oil

Directions

  1. Preheat the oven to 425 degrees F
  2. In a skillet over high heat cook the chicken in oil for 6 to 7 minutes. Be careful not to overcook.
  3. Evenly distribute the chicken, sauce, and onions over the pita 'pizzas'. Top each pizza with a slice of provolone. Place on to a baking sheet and bake for 10-12 minutes until the cheese is melted and bubbly.
