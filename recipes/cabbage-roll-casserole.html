Cabbage Roll Casserole

Cabbage Roll Casserole is a twist on a delicious classic. All the flavor you expect from cabbage rolls but with less work. It is sure to become a family favorite.

Ingredients

  • 2 pounds ground beef
  • 3 1/2 pounds chopped cabbage
  • 1(29 ounce) can tomato sauce
  • 1 cup chopped onion
  • 1 cup uncooked white rice
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 2 (14 ounce) cans beef broth

Steps

  1. Preheat your oven to 350 degree F
  2. In a large skillet over medium-high heat cook the ground beef until browned and crumbly ~5-7 minutes. Drain the grease.
  3. In a large mixing bowl add cabbage, tomato sauce, rice, onion, and salt. Add the ground beef and stir. Pour mixture in to a baking dish and pour beef broth on top.
  4. Cover and bake for 1 hour. Stir, re-cover, and bake until cabbage is tender and rice is cooked ~20-30 minutes.
