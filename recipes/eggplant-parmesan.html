Eggplant Parmesan

This delicious eggplant parmesan is sure to win over the biggest meat fans.

Ingredients

  • 3 large eggplant, peeled and thinly sliced
  • 2 large beaten egss
  • 4 cups Italian seasoned bread crumbs
  • 6 cups spaghetti sauce
  • 1 (16 ounce) package shredded mozzarella
  • 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1/2 teaspoon dried basil

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 350 degrees F
  2. Dip eggplant slices in beaten egg, then in bread crumbs to coat place in a single layer on a baking sheet.
  3. Bake in the for 5 minutes. Flip and bake for another 5 minutes.
  4. In a 9x13-inch baking dish, spread spaghetti sauce to cover the bottom. Place a layer of eggplant slices in the sauce. Sprinkle with mozzarella and parmesan. Repeat layers with remainig eggplant, sauce, and cheese. End with a cheese layer. Sprinkle basil on top.
  5. Bake in the oven until golden brown, aproximately 35 minutes.
